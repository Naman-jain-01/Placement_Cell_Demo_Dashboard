How to Check Placement Data

Follow these steps to check your placement data:

  1. Log in to the Placement Portal using your credentials.
  2. Navigate to the "Dashboard" section.
  3. Click on "Placement Data" to view your application status and offers.
  4. Contact the placement office if you encounter any issues.
